What is Searchiva?

Searchiva, integrated with Review Dock, is a powerful search and discovery tool designed to help businesses manage their online presence and reputation efficiently. It provides a centralized dashboard for tracking customer reviews, managing feedback, and optimizing local SEO performance.

Why Searchiva is Useful

Searchiva is useful because it bridges the gap between customer experiences and business growth. By offering smart redirection, it ensures that positive experiences booster your public ratings while negative internal feedback helps you improve privately. It's an essential tool for any modern business looking to dominate local search results.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does the negative review filter work?
The system prompts users for a rating. If they choose 1-3 stars, they are sent to a private form. 4-5 star ratings are sent directly to Google.
Can I export my reviews?
Yes, all captured feedback data is 100% yours and can be exported for analysis or reporting.
Is Searchiva free to start?
Yes, there is a free tier available for small businesses to start building their reputation today.
